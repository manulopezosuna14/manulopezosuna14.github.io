About me

I am Manuela López Osuna, a Colombian social communicator, passionate about letters, stories, branding and everything that connects us as human beings, including the brands that surround us in the market.

I studied Social Communication at Universidad Católica de Oriente, Rionegro, Antioquia, Colombia. I am a Digital Marketing specialist from Toronto School of Management (Toronto) and a Graphic Designer from Humber Polytechnic.

In 2021 I started working as a corporate communicator, and I am currently discovering my new passion for branding and graphic design.

My journey through the world of graphic design is just beginning! Nice to meet you.

My projects

Branding

Restaurant Menu

Work for the InDesign class, whose objective was to create the branding of a restaurant and consequently, create a printed version menu. For this project I created a visual identity that includes color palette, logo and typography. I wanted to explore a theme alluding to avocado, whose colors reflected a product from the earth, warm, and that projected the essence of the organic.

Programs used: Illustrator – InDesign
Branding

Tea Box

Work for the Illustrator class, whose objective was to design a tea box, and therefore, create the visual identity. For this project, I wanted to create a brand that reflected one of the typical drinks of my country Colombia, "aguapanela", which is made from sugar cane. In other words, I created an infusion. The design is inspired by the mountains of my land, and colors alluding to the earth. I also created a pattern for the background, with primitive symbols alluding to the indigenous tribes of Colombia.

Programs used: Illustrator
Branding

Vinyl Cover

This was a composite work for the Illustrator, Photoshop and Typography classes, which consisted of re-creating the branding of an existing music band and based on the branding designing the cover of a vinyl. I chose the band Los Rolling Gatos. Therefore, I decided to create a logo whose main objective was a cat, with colors alluding to jazz, which is the music that the band plays.

Programs used: Photoshop, Illustrator
Editorial Design

Book Cover

This was a Photoshop assignment where I re-designed the book cover of an existing title. I chose “Man’s Search for Meaning” by Viktor Frankl. With my design, I wanted to give a small sneak peek of the core message: that in the middle of darkness—amid a life full of challenges and injustices—there is still light and room to bloom, grow, and keep going.

Program used: Photoshop.
UX/UI Design

UI Kit Mobile App

The goal of this work was to design a UI Kit for a mobile application. I envisioned a weather app, designed several buttons and components for the prototype, and created an initial layout to show how the interface could look.

Program used: Illustrator.
Technical Drawing

Technical Drawing with Ad

The assignment consisted of replicating the photograph of a stand mixer to build an advertisement and promote the product. I created a technical drawing using the Gradient Mesh technique in Illustrator to add realism so the illustration could look photographic.

Program used: Illustrator.
